# Research Plan: blogging-content-strategy-weapon

- **Depth tier:** normal
- **Time window:** 2025-11-20 back to 2026-05-20 (6 months, with selective reach to early 2025 for canonical references)
- **Page budget target:** 15-20 sources minimum, targeting ~20 high-signal sources
- **Source breadth target:** practitioner blogs, SEO tool documentation, industry reports, case studies, copywriting resources

## Research objectives

This weapon supports `blogging-content-strategy-guardian`, an Angel for founders and indie hackers who need to build topical authority without becoming full-time content marketers. The research must ground the weapon in current (2025-2026) practice across seven domains:

1. Cluster + pillar topical authority architecture
2. Post-length heuristics by search intent
3. Title, H1, and meta description optimization
4. Keyword research without obsession (including free alternatives)
5. AEO / answer-engine formatting for featured snippets and AI Overviews
6. CTA copywriting that converts without begging
7. Content review checklists and editorial quality gates
8. Realistic publishing cadence for solo founders and small teams
